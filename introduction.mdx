## What is ShipKit?

ShipKit is a collection of 300+ TypeScript boilerplates for Astro and Next.js with built-in authentication, Stripe payments, and support for multiple databases and frameworks, enabling you to launch quickly with your favorite tech.

## Get a ShipKit account

To download a TypeScript boilerplate, simply [sign up](https://shipkit.app/sign-up) for a ShipKit account. After a one-off purchase of ShipKit, you'll be able to choose from over 300 boilerplates.
